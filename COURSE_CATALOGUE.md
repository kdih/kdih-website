## PROGRAM DELIVERY MODEL

### Learning Approach

- **Hybrid Learning**: Combination of in-person and online sessions
- **Project-Based**: Real-world projects throughout the program
- **Mentorship**: 1-on-1 mentorship with industry professionals

### Class Schedule

- **Weekday Classes**: Monday - Friday (varies by course)
- **Weekend Options**: Available for working professionals
- **Flexible Timing**: Morning, afternoon, and evening sessions

### Assessment Methods

- Continuous assessment (30%)
- Project work (40%)
- Final examination/presentation (30%)

### Certification

All courses include:

- KDIH Certificate of Completion
- Digital badge for LinkedIn
- Verification via KDIH website
