### I didn't receive my welcome email. What should I do?
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5-10 minutes (email may be delayed)
3. Verify you entered the correct email address
4. Contact support@kdih.org with your registration details

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your registered email
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Link is valid for 1 hour
6. Create a new password

### Can I change my membership type?
Yes! Contact support@kdih.org with:
- Your member ID or email
- Current membership type
- Desired membership type
We'll update your account and notify you.

## üí≥ Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
- **Online:** Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve) via Paystack
- **Bank Transfer:** Account details provided upon request
- **Cash:** Pay at our office during business hours
- **POS:** Available at our office

### Is online payment secure?
Yes! We use Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. Your card details are encrypted and never stored on our servers.

### When will I get access after payment?
**Immediately!** After successful online payment:
- Your course enrollment is activated within 2 minutes
- You'll receive a payment confirmation email
- Course materials become accessible in your dashboard

### I made payment but didn't get access. What should I do?
1. Wait 5-10 minutes (processing delay)
2. Check your email for payment confirmation
3. Login to your dashboard and refresh
4. If still not showing, contact support@kdih.org with:
   - Payment reference number
   - Amount paid
   - Payment date/time

### Can I get a refund?
Refund policy:
- **Before course starts:** Full refund minus 10% admin fee
- **Within first week:** 50% refund
- **After first week:** No refund

Contact support@kdih.org with your request.

### Where's my payment receipt?
Receipts are:
- Emailed automatically after payment
- Available for download in your member dashboard
- Can be re-sent on request
