## INTERVIEW STRUCTURE

### 1. Introduction (5 minutes)

- Welcome the candidate
- Introduce interview panel members
- Explain interview structure and duration
- Set expectations and make candidate comfortable

### 2. Ice Breaker (5 minutes)

- General conversation to relax candidate
- Ask about their journey to the interview
- Discuss general interests

### 3. Candidate Background (10-15 minutes)

- Tell us about yourself
- Walk us through your CV
- What attracted you to this position?
- Why do you want to work at KDIH?

### 4. Technical/Role-Specific Questions (20-30 minutes)

Use questions from the role-specific sections below

### 5. Behavioral Questions (15-20 minutes)

- STAR method questions (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Cultural fit assessment

### 6. Scenario/Case Questions (10-15 minutes)

- Role-specific scenarios
- Problem-solving assessment

### 7. Candidate Questions (10 minutes)

- Allow candidate to ask questions
- Assess their preparation and interest

### 8. Closing (5 minutes)

- Explain next steps
- Thank the candidate
- Confirm contact information

**Total Duration:** 60-90 minutes

## ROLE-SPECIFIC INTERVIEW QUESTIONS

### Hub Manager / Operations Lead

**Technical/Experience Questions:**

1. Describe your experience managing operations in a similar organization
2. How do you prioritize competing demands and manage multiple projects?
3. What strategies have you used to improve operational efficiency?
4. How do you build and maintain partnerships with stakeholders?
5. Describe your experience with budget management
6. How do you motivate and supervise a diverse team?

**Scenario Questions:**

1. You have limited resources but multiple urgent priorities. How do you decide what to tackle first?
2. A key partner threatens to withdraw support. How would you handle this?
3. Your team is underperforming. What steps would you take?

**Behavioral Questions:**

1. Tell me about a time you successfully implemented a new process
2. Describe a situation where you had to manage a difficult stakeholder
3. Give an example of how you've led a team through change

---

### Admin & Finance Officer

**Technical/Experience Questions:**

1. What accounting software are you proficient in?
2. Explain your experience with financial reporting
3. How do you ensure accuracy in your work?
4. Describe your experience managing procurement processes
5. How do you handle confidential financial information?
6. What do you know about Nigerian tax and compliance requirements?

**Scenario Questions:**

1. You discover a discrepancy in financial records. What do you do?
2. A vendor is demanding immediate payment but you haven't received approvals. How do you handle this?
3. You're asked to prepare an urgent report but are missing key data. What's your approach?

**Practical Test:**

- Excel test (basic formulas, pivot tables, data analysis)
- Financial reconciliation exercise

**Behavioral Questions:**

1. Tell me about a time you caught an error that others missed
2. Describe a situation where you had to work under tight deadlines
3. How do you handle competing priorities?

---

### ICT / Technical Support Officer

**Technical/Experience Questions:**

1. What operating systems are you comfortable troubleshooting?
2. Describe your experience with network setup and management
3. How do you approach a technical problem you've never encountered?
4. What cybersecurity measures would you implement for a small organization?
5. Explain your experience with hardware maintenance
6. How do you stay updated on technology trends?

**Scenario Questions:**

1. The Wi-Fi goes down during an important training session. What do you do?
2. Multiple users report computer issues simultaneously. How do you prioritize?
3. You suspect a security breach. Walk me through your response

**Practical Test:**

- Troubleshoot a staged computer problem
- Basic networking setup exercise
- Software installation and configuration

**Behavioral Questions:**

1. Tell me about a complex technical issue you resolved
2. Describe a time you had to explain technical concepts to non-technical users
3. How do you handle frustrated users experiencing technical issues?

---

### Programs & Training Coordinator

**Technical/Experience Questions:**

1. Describe your experience coordinating training programs or events
2. How do you manage multiple programs running simultaneously?
3. What tools do you use for program planning and tracking?
4. How do you measure program success?
5. Describe your experience working with trainers and facilitators
6. How do you handle participant feedback?

**Scenario Questions:**

1. A trainer cancels 24 hours before a workshop with 50 registered participants. What do you do?
2. Participants complain that a program isn't meeting their expectations. How do you respond?
3. You're tasked with launching a new program with a tight deadline and limited budget. How do you approach this?

**Behavioral Questions:**

1. Tell me about a successful program you coordinated from start to finish
2. Describe a time when a program didn't go as planned. What did you learn?
3. How do you ensure all logistical details are covered?

---

### Front Desk / Customer Services Officer

**Technical/Experience Questions:**

1. Describe your customer service experience
2. How do you handle difficult or angry customers?
3. What tools/systems have you used for bookings or registrations?
4. How do you manage multiple phone calls and walk-in visitors?
5. What does excellent customer service mean to you?

**Scenario Questions:**

1. A visitor arrives for a meeting, but there's no record of their appointment. What do you do?
2. You're assisting someone when the phone rings and another visitor walks in. How do you manage?
3. A member complains loudly at the front desk. How do you handle it?

**Role-Play Exercise:**

- Handle a mock customer complaint
- Process a booking request
- Manage multiple simultaneous requests

**Behavioral Questions:**

1. Tell me about a time you went above and beyond for a customer
2. Describe a situation where you dealt with a difficult person
3. How do you stay professional under stress?

---

### Part-Time Trainers & Consultants

**Technical/Experience Questions:**

1. Describe your expertise in [specific skill area]
2. What training or teaching experience do you have?
3. How do you make complex topics easy to understand?
4. What's your approach to hands-on, practical training?
5. How do you assess whether participants are learning?
6. Describe your experience with [specific tools/technologies]

**Scenario Questions:**

1. Half the class is struggling while the other half is bored. How do you handle this?
2. A participant challenges your expertise during a session. What do you do?
3. You need to design a 3-day intensive workshop on [topic]. Walk me through your approach

**Practical Demonstration:**

- Deliver a 10-15 minute mini-lesson on their area of expertise
- Demonstrate teaching methodology
- Handle Q&A session

**Portfolio Review:**

- Review work samples or portfolio
- Discuss previous projects
- Assess depth of expertise

---

### Facility & Operations Assistant

**Technical/Experience Questions:**

1. Describe your experience with facility maintenance
2. What safety protocols are you familiar with?
3. How do you prioritize maintenance tasks?
4. Do you have experience with generators or power systems?
5. How do you handle emergency situations?

**Scenario Questions:**

1. You notice a potential safety hazard. What do you do?
2. Multiple facility issues arise on the same day. How do you prioritize?
3. Equipment breaks down during an important event. What's your response?

**Practical Assessment:**

- Identify basic maintenance issues
- Demonstrate setup of event space
- Show understanding of safety procedures

**Behavioral Questions:**

1. Tell me about a time you identified and fixed a problem before it became serious
2. Describe a situation where you had to work independently
3. How do you ensure quality in your work?

## GENERAL BEHAVIORAL QUESTIONS (All Positions)

1. **Teamwork:** Tell me about a time you worked effectively as part of a team
2. **Problem-Solving:** Describe a challenging problem you solved and how
3. **Initiative:** Give an example of when you took initiative without being asked
4. **Learning:** Tell me about a time you had to learn something new quickly
5. **Conflict:** Describe a conflict with a colleague and how you resolved it
6. **Failure:** Tell me about a professional failure and what you learned
7. **Pressure:** How do you perform under pressure? Give an example
8. **Communication:** Describe a time you had to communicate complex information
9. **Adaptability:** Tell me about a time you had to adapt to significant change
10. **Values:** What are your core professional values and how do they align with KDIH?
