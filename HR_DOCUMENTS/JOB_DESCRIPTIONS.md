# KDIH Job Descriptions

**Official Job Descriptions for All Positions**

## 1. Hub Manager / Operations Lead

### Position Overview

The Hub Manager will oversee all daily operations of Katsina Digital Innovation Hub, ensuring seamless delivery of services across training, coworking, and startup incubation programs.

### Key Responsibilities

- Lead and coordinate all hub operations and activities
- Supervise staff teams and ensure high-quality service delivery
- Oversee training programs, coworking space, and startup incubation activities
- Manage partnerships with government, private sector, and development organizations
- Develop and implement operational processes, policies, and growth strategies
- Monitor budget and financial performance
- Ensure compliance with organizational standards and regulations
- Represent KDIH at events and stakeholder meetings
- Prepare monthly and quarterly reports for management

### Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Management, Technology, or related field
- Minimum 3 years of experience in operations management, tech hubs, education, or program management
- Strong leadership and team management skills
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
- Strategic thinking and problem-solving mindset
- Proven track record of achieving operational targets
- Proficiency in MS Office and project management tools

### Desired Qualifications

- Master's degree in relevant field
- Experience in innovation hubs or tech ecosystem
- Knowledge of entrepreneurship and startup ecosystem
- Grant management experience

### Reporting Structure

Reports to: Executive Director  
Supervises: All hub staff

### Employment Type

Full-time, On-site  
Location: Katsina, Nigeria

## 2. Admin & Finance Officer

### Position Overview

The Admin & Finance Officer will manage financial records, administrative tasks, and ensure accurate documentation across all hub activities.

### Key Responsibilities

- Maintain accurate financial records, receipts, and transaction logs
- Process payments, invoices, and financial documentation
- Manage registration and enrollment data for all programs
- Prepare weekly, monthly, and quarterly financial reports
- Support procurement processes and vendor management
- Maintain digital and physical filing systems
- Assist with budget tracking and expense monitoring
- Coordinate with external auditors when required
- Handle petty cash and bank reconciliations
- Support administrative tasks as needed

### Required Qualifications

- HND/BSc in Accounting, Business Administration, Finance, or Economics
- Minimum 2 years of experience in finance or administrative roles
- Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel and accounting software
- High attention to detail and accuracy
- Integrity and ethical handling of finances
- Good organizational and time management skills
- Basic knowledge of financial regulations

### Desired Qualifications

- Professional certification (ICAN, ACCA, or equivalent)
- Experience with QuickBooks or similar accounting software
- Experience in NGO or education sector

### Reporting Structure

Reports to: Hub Manager  
Supervises: None

### Employment Type

Full-time, On-site  
Location: Katsina, Nigeria

## 3. ICT / Technical Support Officer

### Position Overview

The ICT/Technical Support Officer will ensure all technical infrastructure, equipment, and systems are operational and well-maintained to support training and coworking activities.

### Key Responsibilities

- Manage and maintain computers, Wi-Fi network, printers, and other equipment
- Provide technical support during training sessions and events
- Set up devices and equipment for workshops and programs
- Troubleshoot hardware and software issues
- Maintain system backups and implement basic cybersecurity measures
- Monitor network performance and connectivity
- Manage IT inventory and equipment records
- Install and configure software as needed
- Provide user support and training on technology tools
- Coordinate with external IT vendors when required

### Required Qualifications

- OND/HND/BSc in Information Technology, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related field
- Minimum 1-2 years of experience in IT support or lab management
- Knowledge of Windows and basic networking
- Familiarity with computer hardware troubleshooting
- Good problem-solving and customer service skills
- Ability to work under pressure

### Desired Qualifications

- CompTIA A+, Network+, or similar certifications
- Experience with Linux systems
- Knowledge of cybersecurity best practices
- Experience in educational or training environments

### Reporting Structure

Reports to: Hub Manager  
Supervises: None

### Employment Type

Full-time, On-site  
Location: Katsina, Nigeria

## 4. Programs & Training Coordinator

### Position Overview

The Programs & Training Coordinator will plan, organize, and oversee all training programs, workshops, and incubation activities at KDIH.

### Key Responsibilities

- Plan and coordinate all training programs and workshops
- Liaise with trainers, mentors, facilitators, and participants
- Manage training schedules, attendance, and logistics
- Oversee startup incubation and acceleration programs
- Coordinate participant recruitment and selection processes
- Manage curriculum development and content updates
- Handle certificate generation and distribution
- Prepare program reports and impact assessments
- Coordinate with partners for program delivery
- Manage participant feedback and continuous improvement

### Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Education, Business, Development Studies, or related field
- Minimum 2 years of experience coordinating programs, training, or events
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Strong organizational and project management abilities
- Ability to multitask and work under deadlines
- Proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)

### Desired Qualifications

- Master's degree in relevant field
- Experience in entrepreneurship or innovation programs
- Training or facilitation skills
- Knowledge of monitoring and evaluation frameworks

### Reporting Structure

Reports to: Hub Manager  
Supervises: Program assistants (if any)

### Employment Type

Full-time, On-site  
Location: Katsina, Nigeria

## 5. Front Desk / Customer & Member Services Officer

### Position Overview

The Front Desk Officer will be the first point of contact for visitors, members, and partners, ensuring excellent customer service and smooth front office operations.

### Key Responsibilities

- Welcome and assist visitors, members, and guests
- Handle inquiries via phone, email, and in-person
- Manage coworking desk bookings and class registrations
- Maintain visitor log and member check-in/check-out records
- Provide information about KDIH programs and services
- Handle basic administrative tasks and correspondence
- Maintain cleanliness and organization of reception area
- Coordinate with other staff for visitor needs
- Process payments for walk-in customers (if applicable)
- Manage front desk supplies and inventory

### Required Qualifications

- OND/HND in any field
- 1-2 years of experience in customer service or reception
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Professional and friendly demeanor
- Basic computer skills (MS Office, email)
- Good organizational abilities
- Patience and problem-solving skills

### Desired Qualifications

- Experience in hospitality, education, or tech sector
- Knowledge of basic office management
- Bilingual (English and Hausa)

### Reporting Structure

Reports to: Hub Manager  
Supervises: None

### Employment Type

Full-time, On-site  
Location: Katsina, Nigeria

The Facility & Operations Assistant will support the maintenance of the hub's physical environment and assist with event setup and logistics.

- Manage utilities (water, electricity, waste disposal)

- Good work ethic and reliability

- Basic understanding of building maintenance

### Desired Qualifications

- Technical or electrical knowledge
- Driver's license
- Previous experience in office or educational facility

### Reporting Structure

Reports to: Hub Manager  
Supervises: None

### Employment Type

Full-time, On-site  
Location: Katsina, Nigeria
