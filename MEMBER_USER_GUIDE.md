## üìö Course Enrollment (For Students)

### Browse Courses
1. Visit the **Courses** page
2. View course details, duration, and fees
3. Click **"Enroll Now"**

### Complete Registration
1. Fill out the course registration form
2. Provide personal and professional details
3. Submit form
4. Receive confirmation email

### Make Payment
1. Go to your **Member Dashboard**
2. Find your enrolled course
3. Click **"Pay Now"** button
4. Complete payment via Paystack
5. **Instant LMS access** after successful payment!

### Access Course Materials
1. Login to Member Dashboard
2. Click on your course
3. Access materials, assignments, resources
4. Track your progress automatically

### Track Your Progress
- Progress calculates based on time elapsed
- Formula: (Days since enrollment / Total course days) √ó 100
- View expected completion date
- Download certificate upon completion

## üè¢ Coworking Space (For Coworking Members)

### Your Membership
- Unique **Member Code** issued
- Valid from start date to end date
- Renew before expiry

### Book a Desk
1. Login to Member Dashboard
2. Go to **"Book Desk"** section
3. Choose:
   - Desk type (Hot Desk or Dedicated)
   - Date
   - Duration
4. Submit booking
5. Receive confirmation email

### Check-In
1. Arrive at KDIH
2. Show Member Code at reception
3. Staff will check you in
4. Enjoy your workspace!

### Booking Rules
- Book up to 7 days in advance
- Cancel at least 24 hours before
- One booking per day per member
