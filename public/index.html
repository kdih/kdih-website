About KDIH

Katsina Digital Innovation Hub (KDIH) is a pioneering social enterprise dedicated to driving digital inclusion, youth empowerment, and entrepreneurship across Northern Nigeria.

Based in Katsina City, we provide a collaborative ecosystem combining co-working spaces, digital skills training, startup incubation, and ICT consulting services.

Vision 2030

Position Katsina as Northern Nigeria's innovation hub, creating 1,000 direct and indirect jobs while supporting 100 tech startups.
